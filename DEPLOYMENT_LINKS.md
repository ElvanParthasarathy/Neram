## 📱 Google Play Console Settings
When uploading a new release, ensure these settings are configured:

1.  **App Optimisation**: Set to **ON** (Required for App Bundles/.aab).
2.  **Internal Testing**: Use for immediate 100-user testing without Google Review.
3.  **Data Safety**:
    *   **Data Collected**: Email, User IDs (Account), App performance (Logs), Device IDs.
    *   **Data Usage**: Functional (App functionality), Account Management.
    *   **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/SSL).
