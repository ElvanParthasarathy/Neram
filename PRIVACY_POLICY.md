# Privacy Policy for Neram

**Effective Date: February 11, 2026**

Elvan Parthasarathy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Neram mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
* Email address
* First name and last name
* Register Number (for academic tracking)
* Department, Batch, and Section details

#### Location Data
We may use and store information about your location if you give us permission to do so. We use this data to provide features of our Service and to improve and customize our Service. You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

#### Calendar Data
Our Service integrates with Google Calendar API to sync your academic schedule. We access your calendar data only to display and manage your events within the App. This data is processed as per your sync preferences.

#### Device and Usage Data
We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), unique device identifiers, and diagnostic data (crash logs via Firebase).

## 2. Use of Data
Neram uses the collected data for various purposes:
* To provide and maintain the Service
* To notify you about changes to our Service
* To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service (like notifications)
* To provide customer care and support
* To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
* To monitor the usage of the Service
* To detect, prevent and address technical issues

## 3. Transfer and Disclosure of Data
Your information, including Personal Data, is processed through Firebase (a Google service). We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party companies for marketing purposes.

## 4. Security of Data
The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) provided by Google/Firebase to protect your data in transit and at rest.

## 5. Data Deletion
We provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted. You can use the "Delete Account" feature within the Security Settings of the app to permanently remove your account and all associated data from our servers.

## 6. Service Providers
We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service (e.g., Google Firebase, Google Calendar API). These third parties have access to your Medical Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our Service is intended for students and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
