# Privacy Policy for Neram

**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

Elvan Parthasarathy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Neram mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

*   **Firebase Authentication:** We collect your email address and profile information to manage your account and keep your data synced.
*   **Firebase Realtime Database:** We store your academic preferences (Department, Batch, Section) and notification history to provide a personalized experience.

## 2. Permissions
Our app requires the following permissions to function correctly:
*   **Notifications:** To send you academic updates and reminders.
*   **Exact Alarms:** To trigger notifications at specific class and exam times.
*   **Location:** To provide location-based services (if applicable).
*   **Calendar:** To sync your academic schedule with your device's calendar.

## 3. Data Safety
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use Firebase (a Google service). You can view their privacy policy here: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
