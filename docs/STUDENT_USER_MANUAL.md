## 1. How to Access Neram

Neram works on **every device**. Pick your platform:

### 📱 Android Phone (Recommended)

| Method | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Native App (Best)** | Download the APK from your college or install from Play Store. Open it like any app. |
| **Browser** | Open Chrome → type your college's Neram URL → use it directly |
| **Install as App (PWA)** | Open in Chrome → tap the **⋮** menu (top right) → tap **"Add to Home Screen"** → it appears as a real app on your home screen |

### 🍎 iPhone / iPad

| Method | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Safari (Required)** | Open **Safari** (not Chrome) → type your college's Neram URL |
| **Install as App (PWA)** | In Safari → tap the **Share button** (⬆️ square with arrow) → tap **"Add to Home Screen"** → name it "Neram" → tap Add |

> ⚠️ **iPhone users:** You MUST use Safari for the "Add to Home Screen" feature. Chrome on iPhone does not support installing web apps.

### 💻 Windows PC / Laptop

| Method | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Any Browser** | Open Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Brave → type your college's Neram URL |
| **Install as App** | In Chrome/Edge → click the **install icon** in the address bar (🔽 or ➕) → click "Install" |

### 🖥️ Mac (macOS)

| Method | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Any Browser** | Open Safari, Chrome, or Firefox → type your college's Neram URL |
| **Install as App** | In Chrome → three dots menu → "Install Neram..." → it appears in your Applications |
| **Safari PWA** | Safari → File → "Add to Dock" (macOS Sonoma+) |

### 📱 Admin Mobile App (For Reps & Faculty)

If you're a Class Rep or Faculty Admin, there is a **separate Android app** for the Admin Portal:
- Download the Admin APK from your admin coordinator
- It opens the full Admin Dashboard in a native wrapper
- Supports Google Sign-In and dark/light theme

## 2. Creating Your Account

### Step-by-Step

1. **Open Neram** on any device
2. Tap **"Get Started"** on the welcome screen
3. Choose how to create your account:

#### Option A — Sign Up with Email
1. Tap **"Sign Up"**
2. Enter your **name, college email, and a password** (minimum 6 characters)
3. Tap **"Create Account"**

#### Option B — Sign In with Google (Faster)
1. Tap **"Continue with Google"**
2. Select your Google account (use your college email for best experience)
3. Account is instantly created!

### First-Time Setup

After signing in for the first time, you'll be asked to **choose your academic details**:

| Field | Example | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Batch** | 2023-2027 | Determines your academic year group |
| **Department** | ECE / CSE / MECH | Shows your department's data |
| **Section** | A / B / C | Shows YOUR section's specific timetable |

> ⚠️ **Important:** Choose these correctly! Your timetable, exams, and updates are all based on these selections. If you pick the wrong section, you'll see someone else's schedule.

After selecting, tap **"Continue"** — you're in! 🎉

## 3. Home Screen

Your **daily dashboard** — everything you need at a glance.

### What You See

| Section | What It Shows | Updated By |
|---|---|---|
| **Today's Schedule** | Your classes for today with timings, rooms, and faculty | Admin / Faculty |
| **Daily Update** | Today's announcements (e.g., "Lab cancelled for Period 4") | Class Rep / Faculty |
| **General Notice** | A pinned notice that stays until changed | Class Rep / Faculty |
| **Upcoming Events** | Next few events from your academic calendar | Admin |

### How to Use
- **Pull down** (on phone) to refresh and get the latest data
- The schedule automatically **highlights the current period** with a colored indicator
- If there's no class right now, it shows the **next upcoming class**
- Tap on any event card to see more details

### What the Colors Mean
- 🟢 **Green** highlight = Current period (class happening now)
- 🔵 **Blue** = Regular class
- 🟡 **Yellow** = Special class / Extra period
- 🔴 **Red** = Important notice or exam alert

## 4. Schedule / Timetable

Your **complete weekly timetable** — like having your paper timetable on your phone, but always up-to-date.

### What You See
- A full **Monday to Saturday** grid showing all your periods
- Each period shows: **Subject Name**, **Subject Code**, **Faculty Name**, and **Room Number**
- The **current day** is highlighted automatically
- Time slots are shown in 12-hour format (e.g., 8:20 AM – 9:10 AM)

### Desktop vs Mobile

| | Desktop (Laptop) | Mobile (Phone) |
|---|---|---|
| **Layout** | Full week grid — all 6 days visible at once | One day at a time |
| **Navigation** | Click on any day tab | Swipe left/right to change days |
| **Details** | All info shown inline | Tap a period to see full details |

### Special Classes
If your admin or rep has scheduled special/extra classes, they appear with a distinctive marker so you know they're not part of the regular timetable.

### Real-Time Updates
If your admin changes the timetable (e.g., a faculty change, room swap), your schedule updates **automatically within 1-2 seconds**. No need to refresh manually.

## 5. Exam Schedules

All your exam timetables — **CT, IA, Model, Semester, and Practical** — organized and sorted by date.

### Exam Types

| Type | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **CT1 / CT2** | Cycle Test 1 & 2 | Short internal tests |
| **IA1 / IA2** | Internal Assessment 1 & 2 | Mid-term assessments |
| **Model** | Model Exam | Full-length practice exam before semester |
| **Semester** | Semester/End Exam | Final university exam |
| **Practical** | Lab Practical Exam | Hands-on lab exams with batch-wise scheduling |

### What You See for Each Exam
- **Exam Title** (e.g., "Cycle Test 1")
- **Date Range** (e.g., March 15 – March 20, 2026)
- **Subject-wise breakdown:**
  - Subject name and code
  - Date
  - Start and end time
  - Portion / Syllabus covered

### For Practical Exams
Practical exams show additional info:
- **Batch** (e.g., Batch 1, Batch 2)
- **Register Number Range** (e.g., 23ECE001 – 23ECE030)
- **Lab** and **Time Slot**

### Sorting
All exams are **automatically sorted by date** — the nearest exam always appears first. You never have to scroll to find what's coming up.

## 6. Academic Calendar

All holidays, college events, and important dates in one interactive calendar.

### Two Views

| View | Best For |
|---|---|
| **Month View** | Quick overview — see dots on event days, tap to see details |
| **List View** | Scrollable list of upcoming events with full descriptions |

### How to Use
- **Tap a date** in month view → see that day's events at the bottom
- **Swipe left/right** → change months
- Tap the **"Today" button** → jump back to today
- **Switch views** using the toggle icons in the top-right corner

### Event Types & Colors
- 🔴 **Holiday** — College closed (Pongal, Diwali, etc.)
- 🟢 **Working Day** — Regular academic day with working day number
- 🔵 **Event** — Special activities, cultural events, seminars
- 🟡 **Exam** — Exam dates marked from the exam schedule

### Where Does This Data Come From?
Your college admin uses the **Elvan Agazhi** system to digitize the official printed academic calendar PDF and push it to the app. Any updates are reflected instantly.

## 7. Notes & Study Materials

Browse and view **study materials, PDFs, and documents** shared by your faculty and admins.

### How It Works
- Notes are organized in **folders** (like a file manager)
- Folders are typically organized by **subject** or **unit**
- Tap a folder → go inside it
- Tap a **PDF file** → opens in the **built-in PDF viewer** (no download needed!)
- Use the **back button** (← arrow) to go up one folder level

### Built-in PDF Viewer Features
- **Pinch to zoom** on mobile
- **Scroll through pages** smoothly
- **Full-screen mode** for distraction-free reading
- Works offline if the file was previously loaded

### Can Students Upload Notes?
Currently, only admins and faculty can upload materials. If you have useful notes to share, send them to your class rep or faculty coordinator.

## 9. Account Types & Roles

Neram has **four account types** with different levels of access:

### For Students (You)

| Role | What You Can Do | How to Get It |
|---|---|---|
| **Student** | View timetable, exams, calendar, notes, settings | Automatic — every registered user starts as a Student |
| **Class Rep (Rep)** | Everything a student can do **+ post updates, manage section schedule** via Admin Portal | Your faculty/admin assigns this role to you |

### For Faculty & Administration

| Role | What They Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Faculty Admin** | Manage timetables, exams, events, notes for their department |
| **Super Admin** | Full control over everything — all departments, all sections, all settings |

### How to Know Your Role
Go to **Settings → Profile** — your role is displayed below your email.

## 10. For Class Reps — Admin Access

If you're a **Class Rep (Rep)**, you have access to the **Admin Portal** in addition to the student app.

### How to Access the Admin Portal

| Platform | How |
|---|---|
| **Desktop** | Click **"Admin Portal"** in the left sidebar |
| **Mobile (Browser)** | Tap the **☰ menu** → look for the Admin link |
| **Admin Android App** | Use the separate Admin APK (if provided by your coordinator) |

### What You Can Do as a Rep

| Tool | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Schedule Manager** | Edit YOUR section's timetable (add/change/remove periods) |
| **Exam Manager** | Publish exam schedules for your section |
| **Live Updates** | Post daily announcements that appear on every student's home screen |
| **Event Manager** | Create events for your section |
| **Special Classes** | Schedule extra/remedial classes |

### What You CANNOT Do as a Rep
- ❌ Edit other sections' schedules
- ❌ Access Calendar Manager
- ❌ Manage users or roles
- ❌ Access Archive Tool
- ❌ Access Faculty Directory settings

### Two Apps — When to Use Which

| Situation | Use This |
|---|---|
| Checking your own timetable, exams, calendar | **Student App** (blue icon) |
| Posting updates, editing schedule, publishing exams | **Admin Portal / Admin App** (green icon) |

> 💡 **Tip:** You can have both apps installed on your phone. Use the Student App for daily viewing and the Admin App when you need to make changes.

## 12. Dark Mode & Themes

Neram supports **three theme modes** across all platforms:

| Mode | What Happens | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| ☀️ **Light** | White/light gray background | Daytime, outdoor use, bright rooms |
| 🌙 **Dark** | True black/dark background | Nighttime, OLED screens (saves battery), eye comfort |
| 🔄 **Auto** | Follows your device's system setting | Set it and forget it — theme changes automatically |

### How to Change Theme
1. Go to **Settings** (⚙️)
2. Tap **"Display"**
3. Select **Light**, **Dark**, or **Auto**

### How It Syncs
- **Web → Android:** If you change theme on the web, the Android native app picks it up via NativeBridge
- **Android status bar** changes color to match (black in dark mode, light gray in light mode)
- **iPhone status bar** also matches the theme when installed as a PWA

## 13. Troubleshooting & FAQ

### 🔧 Common Issues

#### "My timetable is wrong / showing someone else's schedule"
**Fix:** Go to **Settings → Profile** and check your **Batch, Department, and Section**. If they're wrong, contact your class rep or admin to correct it.

#### "I can't see any calendar events"
**Fix:** Your admin may not have imported the calendar for your batch yet. Ask your class rep or admin to check the Calendar Manager.

#### "The app shows old data"
**Fix:** Pull down to refresh (on phone) or press Ctrl+R / Cmd+R (on desktop). If it still shows old data, go to **Settings → Storage → Clear Cache**.

#### "I forgot my password"
**Fix:** On the login screen, tap **"Forgot Password"** to reset it via email. Or sign in with Google if your account is linked.

#### "Google Sign-In doesn't work"
**Fix:** Make sure you're using the correct Google account. Check your internet connection. If on iPhone, use Safari instead of Chrome.

#### "I'm a Rep but I can't see the Admin Portal"
**Fix:** Your admin needs to assign the "Rep" role to your account in the Admin Role Manager. Contact your faculty coordinator.

#### "Push notifications aren't working" (Android)
**Fix:** 
1. Go to phone Settings → Apps → Neram → Notifications → make sure they're enabled
2. Make sure battery optimization for Neram is turned OFF
3. Make sure you have internet connection

#### "The app takes too long to load"
**Fix:** 
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear the app cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache)
3. On slow connections, cached data (offline mode) will load instantly

### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use Neram on iPhone?**
> Yes! Open the Neram URL in Safari → tap Share (⬆️) → "Add to Home Screen". It works like a native app with full-screen mode and theme matching.

**Q: Can I use Neram on multiple devices?**
> Yes! Sign in with the same account on any device. Your data syncs automatically across all devices.

**Q: Does Neram work without internet?**
> **Android App:** Yes — your timetable, exams, and calendar are cached locally. You can view them offline. New data syncs when you reconnect.
> **Web App:** Limited — if you previously loaded data, some cached content may be available. For best offline support, use the Android app.

**Q: How do I switch to Dark Mode?**
> Settings → Display → choose **Dark** or **Auto** (Auto follows your phone/computer's setting).

**Q: How do I become a Class Rep on Neram?**
> Ask your faculty coordinator or current admin to assign the "Rep" role to your account in the Admin Portal.

**Q: Can I change my batch/department/section after setup?**
> Contact your admin — they can update your profile details, or you can edit them in Settings → Profile.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
> Yes. Neram uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication and data storage. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Your password is never stored in plain text.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
> Settings → Security → Delete Account. You'll need to enter your password to confirm. This action is **permanent** — all your data will be removed.

**Q: I signed up with Google but want to add a password. How?**
> Settings → Security → Create Password. This lets you sign in with either Google or email+password.

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
> Only admins and faculty can see your full profile (email, batch, section). Regular students can see your name and profile picture in the User Directory.
