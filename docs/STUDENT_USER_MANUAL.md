# Neram — Student App User Manual

> **Neram** (நேரம்) means "Time" in Tamil. It is the official academic companion app for RMK Group of Institutions, designed to keep you on top of your college schedule, calendar, exams, and more.

---

## Getting Started

### How to Access Neram

| Platform | How to Open |
|---|---|
| **Android App** | Download from Play Store or install the APK shared by your college |
| **Browser (Phone)** | Visit **neram.vercel.app** in Chrome/Safari |
| **Browser (Laptop)** | Visit **neram.vercel.app** in any browser |
| **Install as PWA** | On Chrome → tap ⋮ → "Add to Home Screen" (works like a real app!) |

### Create Your Account

1. Open Neram → tap **"Get Started"**
2. Tap **"Sign Up"** → enter your college email, name, and password
3. After signing in, you'll be asked to **choose your Batch, Department, and Section**
   - Example: Batch `2023-2027`, Department `CSE`, Section `A`
   - This determines which timetable and calendar you see
4. You're in! 🎉

### Sign In with Google
- You can also tap **"Continue with Google"** to sign in with your Google account instantly

## 🏠 Home Screen

This is your **daily dashboard** — everything you need at a glance.

### What You'll See

| Section | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Today's Schedule** | Your classes for today with timings, room numbers, and faculty names |
| **Daily Update** | Any announcements posted by your class rep or faculty (like "Lab cancelled today") |
| **General Notice** | A pinned notice that stays until changed (like "Internal exam from March 15") |
| **Upcoming Events** | Next few events from your academic calendar |

### How to Use
- **Pull down** to refresh and get the latest data
- Tap on any event to see more details
- The schedule automatically highlights the **current period** so you know what's happening right now

---

## 📅 Schedule Screen

Your **complete weekly timetable** — like having your paper timetable on your phone, but better.

### What You'll See
- A full **Monday to Saturday** grid showing all your periods
- Each cell shows: **Subject Name**, **Faculty**, and **Room Number**
- The **current day** is highlighted automatically
- On mobile, swipe left/right to see different days

### Desktop vs Mobile
| Desktop | Mobile |
|---|---|
| Full week grid visible at once | One day at a time, swipe to navigate |
| Faculty names shown inline | Tap a period to see full details |

---

## 📆 Calendar Screen

Your **academic calendar** — all holidays, events, exams, and important dates in one place.

### Two Views Available

| View | What It Looks Like | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Month View** | Traditional calendar grid with dots on event days | Quick overview of the month |
| **Schedule/List View** | Scrollable list of upcoming events with dates | Seeing what's coming up next |

### How to Use
- **Tap a date** in month view to see that day's events
- **Swipe left/right** to change months
- Tap the **"Today" button** (top right) to jump back to today
- Switch between views using the icons in the top-right corner
- Events are color-coded by type (holidays, exams, activities)

### Where Does Calendar Data Come From?
Your admin team imports the official RMK academic calendar into Neram. Any changes they make appear instantly on your screen.

## 📝 Notes Screen

Browse and view **study materials, PDFs, and documents** shared by your faculty and admins.

### How to Use
- Notes are organized in **folders** (like a file manager)
- Tap a folder to go inside it
- Tap a **PDF file** to open it in the built-in PDF viewer
- Use the **back button** to go up one folder level
- Pull down to refresh and check for new materials

## ⚙️ Settings

Access Settings from the **menu icon** (☰) on the top bar or sidebar.

### Profile
- View your **name, email, batch, department, and section**
- Edit your display name
- Your profile photo comes from your Google account (if linked)

### Display Settings
- **Light Mode** — white background, great for outdoors
- **Dark Mode** — black background, easy on the eyes at night
- **Auto** — follows your phone's system theme automatically

### Security
- **Change Password** — update your login password
- **Linked Accounts** — link or unlink your Google account for quick sign-in

### Language
- Switch the app language between **English**, **Tamil** (தமிழ்), and **Japanese** (日本語)

### Notifications
- Control which push notifications you receive
- Toggle daily schedule reminders on/off

### Storage & Data
- See how much cached data Neram is using
- **Clear Cache** to free up space (the app will re-download data when needed)
- Clear data for a specific date range

### Calendar Settings
- Customize how the calendar displays events
- Choose your preferred default calendar view

### User Directory
- Browse all registered Neram users
- Filter by **batch → department → section**
- See classmates and their profiles

## 🌐 Browser-Specific Features

### Desktop Sidebar
On laptops/desktops, you get a **full sidebar** on the left with:
- Navigation to all pages (Home, Schedule, Calendar, Notes)
- Quick access to College Sites
- Settings link
- Your profile info at the bottom
- Admin Portal link (if you're an admin)

### Mobile Bottom Navigation
On phones (browser or PWA), you get a **bottom navigation bar** with 4 tabs:
- 🏠 Home
- 📅 Schedule
- 📆 Calendar
- 📝 Notes

### PWA (Progressive Web App)
If you add Neram to your home screen:
- It opens **fullscreen** without the browser address bar
- It has its own **app icon** on your home screen
- The **status bar** matches the app theme (light/dark)
- Works on **iPhone, iPad, Mac, Android, and Windows**

---

## 📱 Android App-Specific Features

### Pull to Refresh
- On any screen, **pull down** from the top to refresh data

### Notifications
- Get **push notifications** for daily schedule updates and important announcements

### Offline Support
- Your schedule and profile are **cached locally** — you can still see your timetable even without internet
- When you reconnect, new data syncs automatically

### Back Button
- The Android back button works naturally:
  - **Sub-page → Parent page → Home → Exit app**
  - Inside settings: **Sub-setting → Settings → Home**

### Onboarding
- First-time users see a beautiful **welcome slideshow** explaining the app's features

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: My timetable is wrong / showing someone else's schedule
**A:** Go to Settings → Profile and check your **Batch, Department, and Section**. If they're wrong, contact your class rep or admin to fix it.

### Q: I can't see any calendar events
**A:** Your admin may not have imported the calendar for your batch yet. Ask your class rep to check.

### Q: The app shows "Offline"
**A:** You don't have internet. Your cached schedule will still show, but new updates won't appear until you reconnect.

### Q: How do I switch to Dark Mode?
**A:** Settings → Display → choose **Dark** or **Auto** (follows your phone's setting).

### Q: I forgot my password
**A:** On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" to reset it via email. Or sign in with Google if your account is linked.

### Q: Can I use Neram on iPhone?
**A:** Yes! Open `neram.vercel.app` in Safari → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". It works just like a native app.

### Q: How do I become a Class Rep on Neram?
**A:** Ask your current admin to assign the "Rep" role to your account in the Admin Portal.
